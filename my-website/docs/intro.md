# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics: An Introduction

## Welcome to the Future of Robotics

Welcome to "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics," a comprehensive textbook designed to guide you through the cutting-edge intersection of artificial intelligence and embodied systems. This textbook explores how modern AI techniques are being integrated into physical robotic systems, particularly humanoid robots, to create machines capable of interacting with the world in human-like ways.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook is structured to take you through the essential concepts, tools, and techniques needed to understand and develop humanoid robotic systems:

- **ROS 2 Fundamentals**: The backbone of modern robotic applications (Chapter 01)
- **Simulation Technologies**: Gazebo and Unity for robot development (Chapter 02)
- **NVIDIA Isaac**: Perception, navigation, and manipulation systems (Chapter 03)
- **VLA and LLM Integration**: Voice-controlled robotic systems (Chapter 04)
- **Capstone Project**: Building an autonomous humanoid robot (Chapter 05)
- **Ethics and Safety**: Critical considerations for human-robot interaction (Chapter 06)

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for:
- Computer science and engineering students
- Robotics researchers and practitioners
- AI/ML engineers interested in embodied intelligence
- Anyone curious about the future of human-robot interaction

## Prerequisites

While this textbook is comprehensive, having basic knowledge of:
- Programming (Python preferred)
- Linear algebra and calculus
- Basic robotics concepts
- Machine learning fundamentals

will enhance your learning experience.

## How to Use This Textbook

Each chapter builds upon previous concepts while remaining as self-contained as possible. We recommend following the chapters in order for the best learning experience, but advanced readers may jump to specific topics of interest.

Let's begin our journey into the fascinating world of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.
